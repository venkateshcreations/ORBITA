Broadcast

Broadcast Infrastructure

Continental-Scale
Content Delivery

ORBITA's dedicated broadcast payload at 68.5°E provides coverage across Europe, Africa, the Middle East, and South Asia from a single orbital slot. Fourteen high-power Ku-band transponders (150 W each) deliver 800+ standard-definition or 200+ high-definition channels simultaneously using DVB-S2X with 256APSK modulation — achieving 3.5 bits/Hz spectral efficiency.

Our next-generation platform supports MPEG-H audio, Ultra HD (4K/8K), and IPTV overlay for hybrid broadcast-broadband receivers. Conditional access systems include Verimatrix, NAGRA, and Irdeto integration with real-time entitlement management.
