Collaboration Framework

How We Work with Agencies

ORBITA's partnership model is built on three pillars: infrastructure reciprocity, standards harmonisation, and joint capability development.

🔄

Infrastructure Reciprocity

Partner agencies gain priority access to ORBITA's 32-station ground network. In return, ORBITA integrates partner ground assets into its global routing fabric.

📋

Standards Harmonisation

Active participation in CCSDS working groups for DTN, CFDP, and space link extension protocols. Ensuring seamless cross-agency data exchange at every layer.

🚀

Joint Development Programs

Co-funded R&D initiatives in optical comms, quantum key distribution for space links, and next-generation software-defined transponder architectures.

📡

Spectrum Coordination

Bilateral frequency coordination agreements filed with the ITU. Shared use of C, Ku, Ka, and V-band allocations across common coverage regions.
