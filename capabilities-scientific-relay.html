Science Data Transport

Downlinking
Discovery

ORBITA's scientific relay service is purpose-built for the unique demands of research and exploration missions. Our ground stations support X-band (8.0–8.4 GHz) and S-band (2.2–2.3 GHz) reception with cryogenically cooled low-noise amplifiers achieving receiver noise temperatures below 20 K. Data rates reach 1.2 Gbps for LEO Earth observation downlinks and 150 Mbps for deep-space probes at Lunar distance.

All relay services comply with CCSDS Space Packet Protocol, including Proximity-1 for Mars relay operations and CFDP for file delivery. Our TDRS-compatible payload enables bent-pipe relay for LEO satellites outside ground station line-of-sight.
