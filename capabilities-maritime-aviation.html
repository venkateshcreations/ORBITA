Global Mobility

Seamless Coverage
Across Every Route

ORBITA's multi-orbit architecture provides uninterrupted connectivity from 70°N to 70°S — covering every major shipping lane, air route, and remote anchorage. Our LEO shell delivers sub-30 ms latency for real-time vessel monitoring and inflight services, while GEO backhaul ensures consistent throughput during polar transitions and beam handovers.

Maritime terminals feature 3-axis stabilized antennas compatible with all vessel classes — from fishing trawlers to ultra-large container ships. Aviation solutions use electronically steerable phased-array panels with zero moving parts, certified for all commercial airframes.

Atlantic Corridor
Full Ka/Ku coverage from 65°N to 55°S. Redundant satellite crosslinks across the mid-Atlantic gap. Marine VSAT with 99.95% availability.

LEO shell coverage from Tokyo to Sydney. Seamless handover across 18 orbital planes. Sub-25 ms latency for real-time cargo tracking.
