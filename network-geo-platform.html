Geostationary Orbit

GEO Relay
Platform

A sovereign geostationary relay platform positioned at 68.5°E, 35,786 km above the equator. Full hemisphere coverage, 24-hour persistent visibility, and high-throughput broadcast and bent-pipe relay for critical government and enterprise missions.

GEO Platform Specifications

Spacecraft & Coverage

Based on a large-class GEO bus with 18 kW power, hybrid Ku/Ka/C-band payload, and designed for 15+ year orbital life. The platform hosts transponders for broadcast, broadband, and secure relay in a single orbital slot.

🌍
Hemisphere Coverage
Fixed 68.5°E orbital slot covers Europe, Africa, Middle East, and South Asia with steerable spot beams for regional concentration and reallocation.

Payload Configuration
48 × 36 MHz Ku-transponders, 24 × 72 MHz C-band, 12 × 500 MHz Ka-beam pairs. Flexible channel switching and bandwidth-on-demand allocation.
