Assured Communications

Hardened for
Critical Missions

ORBITA's secure government service operates across dedicated EHF and AEHF transponders with military-grade encryption at every layer. Our quantum-resistant key distribution protocol (CRYSTALS-Kyber + Dilithium) protects against current and future threats, while null-steering antennas and spread-spectrum waveforms provide Low Probability of Intercept / Low Probability of Detection (LPI/LPD) characteristics.

All ground terminals undergo TEMPEST certification for emissions security. Cross-domain solutions enable seamless data flow between classified (up to TS/SCI) and unclassified networks without physical media transfer.

Encryption
Suite B / Type-1 cryptography with quantum-resistant Kyber-1024 key encapsulation. AES-256-GCM at link layer. All payloads FIPS 140-3 Level 4 certified.
Anti-Jamming
Frequency-hopping spread spectrum across 2 GHz of EHF bandwidth. Null-steering phased array provides 30 dB null depth against hostile emitters.
Resilience
Automatic beam rerouting with <50 ms failover. Dual-homed satellite crosslinks ensure connectivity even after loss of three orbital assets.
Certifications
MIL-STD-810H, MIL-STD-461G, TEMPEST SDIP-27 Level B, NATO STANAG 4631, CCSDS 352.0-B-1 security protocols.
