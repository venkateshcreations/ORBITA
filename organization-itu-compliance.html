Regulatory Framework

ITU Compliance

ORBITA operates under International Telecommunication Union Radio Regulations, CCSDS space data standards, and ISO security certifications. Every satellite, frequency band, and orbital slot is fully licensed and coordinated.

Standards & Certification

Regulatory Compliance Framework

Full compliance with ITU Radio Regulations, Articles 22 (space services) and 9 (coordination). Spectrum filings registered through the ITU BR International Frequency Information System.

📜

ITU Radio Regulations

All orbital slots and frequency assignments are filed with the ITU Radiocommunication Bureau (BR) per Article 22. Advance publication, coordination, and notification procedures completed for 14 satellite networks across LEO, MEO, GEO, and HEO.

🛡️

ISO 17825 Certified

Information security management system certified to ISO/IEC 17825:2024. Cryptographic module testing compliant with FIPS 140-3 Level 2 requirements for all on-orbit command links.

📡

CCSDS Standards

Full adherence to CCSDS 130.0-G (Space Link Extension), CCSDS 727.0 (CFDP), and CCSDS 734.0 (Delay-Tolerant Networking). Interoperable with NASA, ESA, and JAXA mission control centres.

📋

Spectrum Filings

274 individual frequency assignments registered. Coordination agreements signed with 41 administrations. No unresolved interference cases since inception.
