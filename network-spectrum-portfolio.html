ORBITA holds ITU-licensed allocations across L, S, C, Ku, Ka, V, and EHF bands — a comprehensive spectrum portfolio spanning 1 GHz through 300 GHz, supporting every orbital regime and service category.

Allocations & Licensing

Each frequency allocation is coordinated through the ITU Radio Regulations, with priority filings secured via advance publication, coordination, and notification procedures. National licensing in 47 countries complements our global ITU filings.
