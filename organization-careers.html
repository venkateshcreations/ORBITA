Careers at ORBITA

Build the next generation of sovereign space communications infrastructure. We're looking for engineers, operators, and visionaries to deploy and operate the world's most advanced multi-orbit constellation.

Why ORBITA

Culture & Benefits

We operate at the intersection of aerospace engineering, software-defined networking, and international regulation. Our teams span Geneva, California, Bengaluru, and Dubai.

🌍

Global Impact

Your work connects unserved communities, supports disaster response, and enables sovereign communications for allied nations worldwide.

🚀

Mission-Driven

Every role contributes directly to operating 148 satellites and 32 ground stations. You'll see your code and decisions fly in space.

🧠

Innovation First

R&D lab for optical ISL, quantum-safe crypto, and software-defined payloads. Patent incentives and conference attendance supported.

💰

Competitive Equity

Top-quartile salary, performance bonuses, and equity participation in a privately held sovereign infrastructure company.

🏥

Comprehensive Coverage

Full medical, dental, and vision for you and your dependents. Global health insurance for international hires.

✈️

Global Mobility

Rotation programmes between mission centres. Relocation support for inter-office transfers and site assignments.

📚

Continuous Learning

$15K annual learning budget. On-site training at our satellite manufacturing partners and launch provider facilities.
